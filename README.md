## 🚀 How to use

Paste this into your executor and run it (works in autoexec too):

```lua
loadstring(game:HttpGet("https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Dawenese/hub/main/loader.lua"))()
```

Then press **`INSERT`** in-game to open or close the menu.

> 💡 All settings **auto-save** — toggle once and they persist across rejoins and updates.

## ✨ Features

| Tab | What it does |
|-----|--------------|
| **Combat** | Kill Aura • Auto Attack • **Stealth Camp** (fights from under the floor — no damage, hidden from other players) |
| **Zone** | Pick exactly which enemies to farm, per dungeon — search, select-all, collapsible lists |
| **Teleport** | Instant jump to any dungeon (1–7) and the raid bosses |
| **Auto-Play** | One-toggle AFK engine: auto-progresses to your highest dungeon, farms best-XP enemy, dumps stats, equips upgrades, claims rewards, hops |
| **Rolling** | Auto-roll classes until you hit your target (Dragonkin, Celestial, etc.) |
| **Auto** | Auto-claim playtime rewards • auto-equip best gear • auto-upgrade weapon at the blacksmith |
| **Player** | Adjustable walk speed |
| **Stats** | Auto-allocate skill points (Damage / Defense / Health / Gold) |
| **Config** | Anti-AFK • Force Sword • server hop • reset to defaults |

## ❓ Notes

- **Stealth Camp** (Combat tab, on by default) keeps you under the floor while farming — you take no enemy damage and other players just see you on the mob, not floating.
- Visit each dungeon once via the **Teleport** tab so the hub can cache its location — after that, teleports and Auto-Progress work even when a dungeon is streamed out.
- Works on most executors. Settings are stored locally per executor.
